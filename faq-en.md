# Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

---

## Getting Started

<details>
<summary>What happens after I complete registration?</summary>

After confirming your email address, you will be taken to the **Profile Setup** page. Here you set up your professional profile for the first time. Fill in at least your first name, last name, a profile photo and your profession — these are required to appear in Discovery and be visible to other users.

Once your profile is complete, you will automatically be taken to the **Discovery** feed.

</details>

<details>
<summary>Which profile fields are required?</summary>

The following fields are **required**:

- First name and last name
- Profile photo
- Profession

All other fields — company, industry, elevator pitch, hashtags, groups, social links — are optional but strongly recommended. A complete profile significantly increases your chances of meaningful connections.

</details>

<details>
<summary>How do I upload a profile photo?</summary>

On the Profile Setup page and in the **Edit Profile** view, tap your profile picture area. You can then choose an image from your photo library or take a new photo with your camera. enmaru requires access to your camera and photo library for this — you can grant or revoke these permissions at any time in your device settings.

Your photo is stored securely on our servers and is visible to other users within the app.

</details>

<details>
<summary>How do I control who can see my profile information?</summary>

Each optional profile field has a **visibility toggle**. Go to **Edit Profile** and use the eye icon next to each field to show or hide it from other users. Required fields (name, photo, profession) are always visible.

</details>

## Profile

<details>
<summary>How do I edit my profile?</summary>

Open the **Profile** tab at the bottom of the app, then tap the **edit icon** (pencil) in the top right corner. You can update all your profile information here. Changes are saved immediately when you tap the save button.

</details>

<details>
<summary>What are hashtags and groups used for?</summary>

**Hashtags** represent your professional interests (e.g. `#AI`, `#Sustainability`, `#SaaS`). **Groups** are communities or affiliations you belong to (e.g. `@YCombinator`, `@Berlin Founders`).

Both are used in Discovery — other users can search and filter by hashtag or group to find exactly the professionals they are looking for.

</details>

<details>
<summary>What is the Elevator Pitch field?</summary>

The Elevator Pitch is a short personal description — your professional "30-second pitch". It is displayed prominently on your profile and helps others quickly understand who you are and what you do.

</details>

<details>
<summary>How do I set my Trip or Event status?</summary>

In **Edit Profile**, you can activate:

- **Currently Visiting** — signals that you are currently travelling or visiting a city. Shown as a badge on your profile card.
- **Next Event** — enter the name, location and date of an upcoming event you are attending. Other attendees can see this and connect with you in advance.

</details>

## Discover

<details>
<summary>How does Discovery work?</summary>

Discovery shows you professionals within a radius of up to **50 km** around your current location in real time. The feed updates each time you pull to refresh.

To appear in Discovery, you must have **Location Sharing** enabled (Settings → Privacy Settings → Location Sharing). Your exact GPS coordinates are never shared — other users only see a rounded distance (~100 m precision).

</details>

<details>
<summary>Why don't I see any users nearby?</summary>

This can have several reasons:

- **Location Sharing is disabled** — enable it in Settings → Privacy Settings → Location Sharing
- **OS location permission denied** — check your device Settings → Apps → enmaru → Permissions → Location
- **No users nearby** — the Discovery radius is 50 km. In less populated areas there may simply be fewer users active.
- **Account deactivated** — if you have deactivated your account, you will no longer appear in Discovery and the feed may appear limited.

</details>

<details>
<summary>How do I filter the Discovery feed?</summary>

Use the **filter chips** at the top of the Discovery feed:

- **Meet** — shows only users with an active Meet Me check-in
- **Travel** — shows only users currently travelling
- **Event** — shows only users attending an event
- **Favourites** — shows only users you have added to your favourites

You can also use the **search bar** to filter by name, `#hashtag` or `@group`.

</details>

<details>
<summary>Can I switch between grid and list view?</summary>

Yes — tap the **view toggle icon** in the top right corner of the Discovery feed to switch between grid and list view. Your preference is remembered for the current session.

</details>

## Messages

<details>
<summary>How do I start a conversation?</summary>

Open a user's profile by tapping their card in Discovery, then tap **Send Message**. This opens a direct chat thread. You can also access your conversations anytime via the **Messages** tab.

</details>

<details>
<summary>Can I pin important conversations?</summary>

Yes — swipe left on any conversation in the Messages list to reveal the **pin** option. Pinned conversations appear at the top of your list.

</details>

<details>
<summary>How do I mute a conversation?</summary>

Swipe left on a conversation and tap **Mute**. You will no longer receive notifications for that conversation, but you can still read and reply to messages.

</details>

<details>
<summary>Why am I not receiving chat notifications?</summary>

Check the following:

1. **Chat notifications enabled?** — Settings → Privacy Settings → Chat Notifications must be ON
2. **OS notification permission?** — check your device Settings → Notifications → enmaru
3. **Push Notifications enabled?** — Settings → Privacy Settings → All Notifications must also be ON
4. **App in foreground?** — notifications are displayed differently when the app is open

</details>

## Meet Me

<details>
<summary>What is Meet Me?</summary>

Meet Me is enmaru's real-time check-in feature. When you check in to a venue, your location (venue name) becomes visible to nearby professionals. They can see that you are available and where — making spontaneous, in-person networking effortless.

</details>

<details>
<summary>How do I check in to a venue?</summary>

Go to the **Meet Me** tab (location icon), then tap **Select a venue**. The app uses your GPS to show nearby venues. Select your venue, confirm the check-in, and you're visible to others in your area.

You must have Location Sharing enabled to use the venue picker.

</details>

<details>
<summary>How do I set my availability?</summary>

After checking in, you can choose from three availability modes:

- **Now** (open end) — you are available right now with no time limit
- **Now until** — available now until a specific time today
- **Time window** — available during a defined time window (e.g. 14:00–16:00)

</details>

<details>
<summary>Who can see my check-in?</summary>

Your venue name and availability status are visible to all users within your discovery radius. Your exact GPS coordinates are never shared — only the venue name and address you selected are displayed.

You can check out at any time to remove your visibility.

</details>

## Privacy Information

<details>
<summary>What data does enmaru collect?</summary>

enmaru collects only the data necessary to provide the service: your email address, profile information you choose to enter, approximate GPS location (with your consent), and anonymised usage events for product improvement.

For full details, please read our **[Privacy Policy](/legal/privacy-policy)**.

</details>

<details>
<summary>How do I withdraw my consent?</summary>

You can withdraw any consent at any time:

- **Location Sharing** — Settings → Privacy Settings → Location Sharing (toggle off)
- **Push Notifications** — Settings → Privacy Settings → All Notifications (toggle off)
- **Chat Notifications** — Settings → Privacy Settings → Chat Notifications (toggle off)

Withdrawing consent does not affect the lawfulness of processing based on consent before its withdrawal.

</details>

<details>
<summary>How do I download a copy of my data?</summary>

Go to **Settings → Support & Legal → Download my data**. After re-authenticating, you will receive an email with a secure download link (valid for 24 hours). The export includes all your personal data in JSON format (GDPR Art. 20).

</details>

## Account Deletion

<details>
<summary>How do I delete my account?</summary>

Go to **Settings → Delete Account**. You will be asked to confirm twice and re-authenticate (password or "DELETE" confirmation for Google accounts). Deletion is immediate and permanent.

</details>

<details>
<summary>What happens to my data after deletion?</summary>

All your personal data is deleted immediately:

- Profile, photo, profession and contact details
- Location and presence data
- Messages and chat history
- Favourites and connections
- Event, travel and Meet Me status

An anonymised compliance record (no personal data) is retained for up to 3 years as required by GDPR Art. 17(3). You will receive a confirmation email.

</details>

<details>
<summary>Can I reactivate a deleted account?</summary>

No — account deletion is permanent and cannot be undone. All data is erased immediately. If you wish to use enmaru again, you will need to register a new account with a valid invitation code.

If you only want to temporarily hide your profile, use **Settings → Privacy Settings → Account active** (toggle off) instead — this hides you from Discovery without deleting your data.

</details>

## Settings

<details>
<summary>How do I change my email address?</summary>

Go to **Settings → Account → Change email**. Enter your new email address and confirm with your current password. A verification email will be sent to your new address — click the link to confirm the change.

</details>

<details>
<summary>How do I change my password?</summary>

Go to **Settings → Account → Change password**. Enter your current password and then your new password (minimum 8 characters). If you have forgotten your password, use the **Forgot password?** link on the Sign In screen.

</details>

<details>
<summary>What is Dark Mode?</summary>

Dark Mode switches the app's colour scheme to a dark background, which is easier on the eyes in low-light conditions and may reduce battery consumption on OLED screens. Toggle it under **Settings → Profile → Dark mode**, or it follows your device's system setting by default.

</details>

<details>
<summary>What is "Account active" in Privacy Settings?</summary>

When **Account active** is turned off, your profile is hidden from Discovery and you will no longer appear in other users' feeds. You can still use the app, read messages and reactivate at any time. This is useful when you are not available for networking for a period of time.

</details>
