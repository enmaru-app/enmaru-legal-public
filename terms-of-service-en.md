## 2. Service Description

enmaru is a location-based B2B networking platform offering:
- **Discovery:** Find nearby professionals in real time (GPS-based, ~50 km radius)
- **Meet Me / Check-in:** Signal your availability at a venue
- **Direct Messages:** Chat directly with other users
- **Profile:** Manage your professional profile with per-field visibility controls
- **Push Notifications:** Receive alerts for new messages
